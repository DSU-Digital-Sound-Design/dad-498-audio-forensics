Grading notes

- Reward cautious language over exact firearm-model claims.
- Do not require students to name exact caliber or exact model.
- Strong answers should separate:
  - baseline firearm-type differences (pistol, rifle, shotgun)
  - revolver-gap dual impulses
  - shock-wave crack-then-boom timing
  - later reflections or reverberant tail that are not separate source events
- The annotated SVGs in `top_candidates/` are useful for a private walkthrough or future answer key refinement.
